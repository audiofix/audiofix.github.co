About Audiofix

Audiofix is the South West's premier service and repair centre for both modern and retro DJ equipment. After initially specialising in the mighty Technics SL-1200/1210 series turntables, we've since expanded to handle most major brands of turntables, CDJs, DJ mixers, controllers, and other audio equipment.

We keep the focus on high quality workmanship with a quick turnaround, and our customer service keeps our clients happy. Check out our testimonials to hear from our happy customers!

Turntable Service

A turntable service will include but is not limited to;

  • Deep clean inside and out
  • Check soldering on all PCBs and reflow loose connections
  • Components assessed for heat damage
  • Check condition of operation base (33/45/power buttons)
  • Check and calibrate electronic brake
  • Strip, clean and lubricate pitch fader
  • Remove pitch 0% click (if requested)
  • Full pitch fader range and gain calibration
  • Function test under oscilloscope
  • Check and lubricate centre shaft
  • Remove, clean and lubricate target light assembly
  • Check tonearm functions including height adjust, anti-skate and arm lift
  • Calibrate tonearm bearings
  • Check and clean tonearm cartridge contacts
  • Platter cleaned
  • PAT Test and label

We can also upgrade the audio cable and grounding wire for £25 per deck. The cables are custom made in-house 1.2m Pro Grade ultra pure OFC Van Damme, with gold plated Neutrik RCA connectors and a new 24AWG grounding wire with insulated, crimped spade connector bound together.

CDJ Service

A CDJ service will include but is not limited to;

  • Full unit test (with MIDI Mapping software)
  • Deep clean inside and out
  • Replace Play/Pause + Cue button tact switches (parts included)
  • Jog wheel stripped, cleaned and bearings aligned
  • Laser unit and pickup stripped and cleaned
  • Remove and clean all function actuators
  • Clean and lubricate all potentiometers and faders
  • LCD test (if applicable)
  • Deoxidise inputs / outputs
  • Firmware update
  • PAT Test and label

Audiofix Repairs

BEFORE

AFTER

If you just need that little niggle sorted – such as a CDJ Cue button not working, or a mono signal output from a turntable – then an Audiofix repair will get you back up and running.

Our repair process is slightly different to the service process. After we receive your equipment we go through a diagnostic procedure to discover the source of the issue. Once the diagnostic is complete, we'll contact you to give you a report of the issue and a quote to complete the repair.

After you give us the go ahead, it won't be long until your equipment is up and running again, squeaky clean and good as new! For repairs we charge a standard bench fee at the beginning of the job.

For completed Audiofix repairs, this fee is deducted from the labour component of the final invoice.
